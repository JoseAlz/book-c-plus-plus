Assigning %score to pScore
&score is: 0x61ff00

score is :1000

Adding 500 to score
score is: 1500
*pScore is: 1500

Adding 500 to *pScore
score is: 2000

Assigning &newScore to pScore

newScore is: 5000

str is: score
